NRCS Conservation Planning Policy

Direction for applying the planning process is derived from five major sources:

Policy- NRCS conservation planning policy is detailed in theGM 180 P409.pdf

Procedure- The National Planning Procedures Handbook (NPPH), H-180, Part 600, supports the planning policy by describing the planning process and the how-to guidance used by NRCS to carry out that process.  (National Planning Procedures Handbook)

Technical Guidance- The Field Office Technical Guides (FOTG) are the primary technical reference for NRCS and are localized to apply specifically to an identified geographic area.

Tools. User guides for specific tools contain information for use and maintenance of conservation planning tools.

Program Guidance- Manuals contain policy and guidance for administering programs that can facilitate implementation of planned measures. For additional information on providing technical assistance to clients, see the Programs Manual, Title 440, Part 525, Conservation Technical Assistance Program.
